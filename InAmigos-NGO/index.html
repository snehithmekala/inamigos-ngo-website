What is an NGO?

A Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) is a non-profit organization that works independently to improve society by addressing social, educational, environmental, healthcare, and humanitarian issues. NGOs aim to create positive change and support communities through various initiatives and volunteer efforts.

What Does InAmigos Foundation Do?

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive social change. Through initiatives in education, women empowerment, environmental conservation, animal welfare, and community service, the foundation works with passionate volunteers to build a better future for individuals and communities across India.
